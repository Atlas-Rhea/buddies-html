TOURNAMENT RULES OF PLAY

(Read once, break often)

  1. Best Ball Format:

    Each player hits from the spot of the previous "best" shot, as chosen by the team. Democracy in action—sort of.

  2. Team Scoring:

    Record one team score per hole. Lowest total wins. Bragging rights included.

  3. Dress Code:

    You can follow golf rules or position yourself to win the coveted Best Dressed, you choose.

  4. Mulligans Are Fiction:

    Mr. Mulligan was not invited. No do-overs. Play it where it lies… even if it's a flowerbed.

  5. No Gimmies:

    Putt it out. The Golf Gods are watching—and they're petty.

  1. Out of Bounds = Out of Bounds:

    Unless it's faster, funnier, or there's a clear path through the woods. Use judgment. Or don't.

  2. Cart Speed Limits Apply:

    Drive responsibly. We've hired invisible speed enforcement. You've been warned.

  3. Lost Ball Protocol:

    If you can't find it in 90 seconds, it's gone. Declare it lost, curse the squirrels, move on.

  4. Cheating Policy:

    Cheating is strictly prohibited—unless it's clever, and no one finds out. In that case, we admire the hustle.

  5. Mandatory Fun Clause:

    If you're not having fun, that's a you problem. Adjust attitude, refill beverage, and carry on.

Good Luck!
